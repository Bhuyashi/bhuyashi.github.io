Deliciously Rendered: A Blender Donut Project

This project showcases a 3D-rendered donut created in Blender, focusing on realism and aesthetic appeal. The goal was to practice procedural modeling, texturing, and lighting techniques while achieving a visually appetizing result.

Key aspects of the project include:

  • Modeling: The donut was sculpted using Blender’s subdivision and sculpting tools to achieve a soft, doughy appearance.
  • Texturing & Shading: Procedural textures were used for the dough and icing, with subsurface scattering to enhance realism. Sprinkles were generated using particle systems for a natural distribution.
  • Lighting & Rendering: A three-point lighting setup was used to highlight textures and shadows effectively. The final render was done using Cycles for high-quality photorealistic output.
  • Post-processing: Minor adjustments were made in Blender’s compositor to refine colors and contrast.
