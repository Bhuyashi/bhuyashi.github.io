Hey,

I am a first-year Master's student in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research interests broadly lie in Computer Vision and Machine Learning.

Prior to this, I worked in the Multimedia and Algorithms team at Samsung Semiconductor India Research, where I contributed to the image post-processing pipeline in Exynos SoCs, specifically for the Dense Optical Flow engine and the Spatio-Temporal Noise Reduction engine.

Before that, I completed my Dual Degree (B.Tech and M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering from IIT Bombay. For my masters thesis, I was supervised by Prof. Virendra Singh where I worked on building efficient hardware accelerator for convolutional neural network.

In my free time, I enjoy reading and painting. Please feel free to email me with questions or feedback regarding any of my projects.
